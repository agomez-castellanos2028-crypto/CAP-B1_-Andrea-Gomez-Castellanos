Growth

Coffee and Pasteries!

This project is called Coffee and Pasteries where you can find your favorite coffee and matcha places as well as some pasteries recipes.

This project is where I think that I showed the most growth throughout my Computer Science class. This project shows my growth in how my skills in computer science have grown and evolved throughout the school year. From this project, you can see my creativity and the hard work that I put into it. I would say the hardest area for me to master was to be able to put the images in certain places I wanted them on the screen, as well as not being able to identify certain bugs at times. Hardest obstacles were trying not to get stressed when my code wouldn't work, or there was a glitch with my photos, and they were all over the screen in different sizes. How I overcame this was by using my computer science skills and looking through past assignments that would help me fix the code. I am very proud of this project and think maybe the only improvement I could do is fixing my pictures, but overall I think I did very well and presented everything perfectly
